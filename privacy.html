Privacy

Privacy policy.

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Overview

Crunchy Codes projects are designed to avoid collecting personal information unless clearly stated in the relevant project documentation.

Project data

Tools may store local settings or workspace data when needed for functionality. This data stays on your device unless a specific project states otherwise.

Telemetry

Crunchy Codes projects do not collect telemetry by default. If a future project, including an editor extension, uses telemetry or connects to an external service, its documentation will describe what is collected and how it is used.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support details provided on the relevant project repository or documentation.
