Privacy

Privacy policy.

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Overview

Crunchy Codes VS Code extensions are designed to avoid collecting personal information unless clearly stated in the extension documentation.

Extension data

Extensions may store local settings through VS Code configuration or workspace storage when needed for functionality. This data stays on your device unless a specific extension states otherwise.

Telemetry

Crunchy Codes extensions do not collect telemetry by default. If a future extension uses telemetry or connects to an external service, its Marketplace listing and documentation will describe what is collected and how it is used.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support details provided on the relevant extension repository or Marketplace listing.
